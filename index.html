What is an AI Agent?

An AI (Artificial Intelligence) agent is a software program that can perform tasks or provide services on behalf of a user or another program with some degree of autonomy. These tasks often involve decision-making, problem-solving, or automation and can range from simple to complex operations. AI agents are designed to simulate human behavior and intelligence, using techniques such as machine learning, natural language processing, and computer vision.
